The Hale Dynasty · A record assembled from ten volumes

Not myth. Evidence.

An English family held one estate for nearly a thousand years and left behind a record dense enough to outlast memory.

This version of the opening page avoids abstract spectacle. It treats the archive as something physical: copied, folded, witnessed, buried, recovered, and read again.

What makes the story large is not glow or mystery for its own sake. It is the pressure of small things kept for centuries until they become undeniable.
