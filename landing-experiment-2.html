A fictional archive · 1066–2026 · ten volumes

A dynasty needs more than history. It needs a reader.

The Hale Dynasty is a family saga told through chronicles, letters, land records, maps, portraits, and the long administrative afterlife of inheritance. It begins in conquest and ends with a woman in Oxford discovering she has been inside the story all along.

What this page is trying

An elegant exhibit instead of a dark archive

This direction treats the project like a literary exhibition: brighter, grander, more editorial, and more legible at first glance. The archive becomes something you are invited into, rather than something you must decode before it welcomes you.
